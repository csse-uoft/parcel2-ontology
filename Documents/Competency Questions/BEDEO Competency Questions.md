# Competency Questions 
We present in the following the main competency questions identified during our analysis. They help focus the scope of information that is needed to capture and describe the most important components of partnerships.  

## Location-related
- A1) What is the address of the site?  
- A2) What are the individual address components? (e.g., unit number, street name, street number, city name, etc.)  
- A3) What is the geospatial representation of the site/location? (e.g., the geospatial polygon)  
- A4) What are the zoning regulations in place for the site/location?  

## Address-related (from use cases)
- B1) What geographical feature is the location of interest “near of” ?
- B2) What landmark is the location of interest “near of” ?
- B3) What infrastructure “entity” is the location of interest “near of” ?
- B4) What is the relative location of the property in relation to another landmark?
- B5) Where is the location of interest “approximately” located?
- B6) What street is the property located on?
- B7) What is the street number of the property?
- B8) What is the building name of the property?
- B9) What is the locality of the property?
- B10) What is the province/state of the property?
- B11) What is the country of the property?

## Address-related (from standards and guidelines) 
- C1) What is the unit number of the address?
- C2) What is the unit type (e.g., suite, unit, etc.) of the address?
- C3) What is the street number of the address?
- C4) What is the street name of the address?
- C5) What is the street type (e.g., drive, street, boulevard, etc.) of the address?
- C6) What is the additional/supplemental information of the address?
- C7) What is the direction of the street?
- C8) What is the locality of the address?
- C9) What is the province/state of the address?
- C10) What is the country of the address?
- C11) What is the rural route identifier of the address?
- C12) What is the postal station information of the address?

## Asset-related
- E1) Who owns the asset?
- E2) What type of asset is it?
- E3) What is the location of this asset?
- E4) What is the description of this asset?
- E5) Who has an interest in the asset?
- E6) Who is the beneficiary of the asset?
- E7) Does the asset depreciate?
- E9) Is the asset tangible or intangible?
- E12) Is the asset movable or fixed?
- E13) What is the value of the asset?
- E14) Who is leasing the asset?
- E15) Who is using the asset? 
## Land-related
- G2) What type of land/real property?
- G5) Who has an interest in this “Land”?
- G7) What is this “Land” used for (e.g., residential, mixed use, industrial, commercial,...)?
- G9) What is this Land composed of?
- G10) What is the total surface area of this Land ?
- G13) What is this Land’s identifier? - there could be multiple identifiers associated with a Land
- G14) What municipal services does the Land have access to?
- G15) What is the current zoning of this Land?
- G16) What parcels are part of this Land? 

## Parcel-related 
- H7) Who has an interest in this “parcel”?
- H8) What is this “parcel” used for (e.g., residential, mixed use, industrial, commercial,...)?
- H10) What is this parcel composed of?
- H11) What is the total surface area of this parcel?
- H13) What is this parcel’s identifier? - there could be multiple identifiers associated with a parcel
- H14) What municipal services does the parcel have access to?
- H15) What is the current zoning of this parcel?
- H16) What Land is this parcel part of?
- H17) What parcel is this parcel part of?

## Call for Proposals-related
- J1) Where is the development site located? 
- J3) When was the call for proposal published?
- J4) When is the deadline for the call for proposal?
- J5) What are the restrictions/limitations of the project?
- J6) What are the restrictions/limitations of the project participants?
- J7) Are there any additional resources pertaining to the project’s call for proposals (e.g., external websites, files)  
- J8) What applications have been submitted to this call for proposals

## Application-related
- K1) Who is the principal applicant for the application?
- K2) Who is the co-applicant for the application?
- K3) What is the status of the application?
- K4) What is the proposal associated with the application?
  
## Proposal-related 
- L1) What type of development is being proposed for the project?  
  - L1a) What type of land use is being proposed for the project? (e.g., retail, residential, mixed use)
- L2) What accessibility-related standards does the project meet?
- L3) What energy/emissions-related standards does the project meet?
- L4) What affordabillity-related standards does the project meet?
- L5) What is the size/scale of the project?  
  - L5a) What is the height of the proposed building(s)  
  - L5b) How many units are proposed to be built? (e.g., number of housing units)  
  - L5c) How much floor space will be available for use?  
- L6) Are there any additional resources pertaining to the project’s proposals (e.g., external websites, files)

## Project Participant-related
- M1) What is the name of the participant?
- M2) What is the legal status of the participant?
- M3) Who are the participants of the project?
  - M3a) What are the roles/responsibilities/deliverables of each participant?
- M4) Who owns the assets used for the project?
  - M4a) Who owns the land of the development site?
  - M4b) Who will provide funding?
    - M4b1) How much funding?
- M5) How much project-related experience does the participant have?
- M6) What is the financial viability of each participant?

## Restrictions/Limitations on the Project  
- N1) What are the atomic restrictions/limitations of the project, i.e., the restrictions that target a specific characteristic?    
  - N1a) Minimum building size/height is 5 stories 
  - N1b) Maximum building size/height 
  - N1c) Minimum number of units is 210 
  - N1d) Maximum rent price (e.g., for affordable housing) is $2000
  - N1e) Minimum accessibility requirements
  - N1f) Restrictions on energy efficiency and emissions (e.g., for environmental / sustainability compliance)

## Restrictions/Limitations on the Project Participants
- O1) What are the atomic restrictions/limitations of the project, i.e., the restrictions that target a specific characteristic?   
  - O1a) Minimum number of years of experience is 5
  - O1b) Minimum number of similar past projects completed is 2
  - O1c) Minimum debt coverage ratio is 1.10
